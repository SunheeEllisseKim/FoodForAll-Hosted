Welcome

Who We Are:

We are a nonprofit organization committed to finding ways to get food for all people.

Problem Statement:

There is a large amount of food wasted in homes and restaurants everywhere. Some of this food is still edible and can be donated to those who need it. However, it is instead thrown away because of how inconvenient it is to put otherwise wasted food to use. Our goal is to make a convenient and easy-to-use process to promote the donation of this unneeded food.

Our Solution:

We have created a delivery system that allows for people and restaurants to fill out a simple form, and get the food they would like to donate delivered directly from their door to a nearby foodbank. Our website focuses on not only making it easy for those donating, but also those who are doing the delivery. We have implemented a covid reporter as well as a foodbank locater for the drivers to make the driving process efficient.

Q&A

Question: "Why do we need your Twitter handle?
Answer: "The Twitter Handle is not necessary, it is simply an added incentive for those who choose to enter it when they donate. We will be tweeting out a thankyou note to everyone who donates and chooses to participate."

Question: "Where does the food donated go?"
Answer: "The food goes to the nearest foodbank to the donator and is then distributed to the needy by the foodbank."

Question: "Where do the drivers come from?"
Answer: "The drivers are volunteers from the foodbanks and are deployed by our website."

Mission

We here at food for all strive at making this world a better place.

Our hope is to reduce the amount of food wasted as a contribution to society. Thank you so much for supporting our cause.
